ROLF (2021)

Python (Numpy, Flask), HTML, Bootstrap 4

ROLF (Recommendation of Letterboxd Films) is a proof-of-concept recommendation algorithm for Letterboxd users, a site where people can give numerical ratings to films that they have seen. It parses RSS feeds of user profiles to pull existing ratings and uses linear algebra to estimate ratings.

Study Buddy (2020)

Java, Group Project

Study Buddy is a proof-of-concept productivity app for Android that rewards commitment to planned tasks with trust and improved mood of a virtual pet.

My focus was programming the virtual pet, specifically keeping track of its mood, trust level of the user, and these two pet statistics were affected by user behavior.

Aspectum (2019)

C++, Group Project

Aspectum is a proof-of-concept art installation for the Gates and Allen CS buildings at the University of Washington. Aspectum displays the noise levels of selected study spaces in both buildings through diffused LED lights, measured through decibel meters sent over Wi-Fi.

My focus was processing the incoming noise level readings and displaying them visually. This was done via an Arduino.
